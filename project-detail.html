About This Project

I want people to see my work and fall in love with it. I put 100% of all my effort into every piece. I just want to make others happy and hopefully build a big career off of this experience. Drawing has always been a passion of mine, and I want to share that passion with others through my art. I hope to bring joy and positivity to those who see my work!

I have an online persona which is what I use instead of my real face and name. So from time to time My signatures have evolved over time but it is still me. My favorite color is red/pink so that is why my site has that color scheme. I want my site to feel warm and inviting, just like my art!

Key Features

  • Customer can see my artwork.
  • Customer can contact/commission me through the site.
  • Customer can see my prices and learn about me.
  • Site is a not only a Portfolio but a journal/gallery
  • A place where I can store my artwork for future and present use.
